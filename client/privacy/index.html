Blobl.io Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

At Blobl.io, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how it is used:

  • Game Data: We collect data related to your interactions with the game, such as gameplay progress, scores, and other in-game activity.
  • Discord Data: If you choose to connect your Discord account, we collect relevant data such as your username, ID, and interaction details within our Discord community.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to improve your experience on Blobl.io and to provide essential services:

  • Improvement of Services: We analyze gameplay data to optimize game features and enhance user experience.
  • Communication: We may use your information to send you important updates, notifications, or announcements related to Blobl.io.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your personal information and employ industry-standard measures to protect it from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or in cases where third-party services are essential for the operation of Blobl.io (e.g., hosting, analytics services). In such cases, we ensure that the third parties comply with applicable privacy regulations.

Children's Privacy

Blobl.io is designed for a general audience and does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. All changes will be posted on this page with the updated date. Continued use of Blobl.io after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
